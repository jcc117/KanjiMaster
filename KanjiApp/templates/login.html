What is KanjiMaster?

KanjiMaster is an interactive online study tool to study the Japanese written language, one notoriously difficult for English speakers to pick up on. This app makes the process of studying hiragana, katakana, and kanji into an interactive fun process by quizzing the user on various compounds based on difficulty level. Progress reports can give you a baseline on how well you are doing what you might need to work on.

This site is the labor of love of Jordan C. Carr, a computer science and Japanese major at the University of Pittsburgh. It was started as a study aide and practice developing effective web applications. It is still a work in progess.
